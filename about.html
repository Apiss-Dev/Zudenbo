About Us

Electrical and mechanical installations play a vital role in development, serving as infrastructure for building or industrial construction.

As an electrical and mechanical contractor, civil engineering, and engineering firm, Zudenbo Cakra Utama has contributed to development in Indonesia.

Zudenbo Cakra Utama was established in February 2026.

Supported by strong human resources programs and continuous development of engineering and managerial skills, we are committed to providing the best service and support to achieve our clients' project goals.
